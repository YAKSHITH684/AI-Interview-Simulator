Hand-picked practice platforms, guides, books, and communities to back up your roadmap — organized by what you're working on right now.

🔍
💻 Coding & DSA Practice
LeetCode The most widely used coding interview practice platform — thousands of problems, many tagged by company. NeetCode Curated problem lists (like the NeetCode 150) with clear video walkthroughs for core patterns. HackerRank Practice problems plus real skill-certification tests some employers accept directly. GeeksforGeeks Deep reference articles on data structures, algorithms, and company-specific interview experiences. InterviewBit Structured, level-based DSA tracks aimed specifically at interview prep. Codewars Bite-sized "kata" challenges — good for daily practice and language fluency.
🏗️ System Design
System Design Primer Free, thorough open-source guide covering scalability fundamentals — the standard starting point. ByteByteGo Visual explainers and newsletter breaking down real-world system architectures. Educative — Grokking System Design Paid, structured interactive course walking through classic system design interview questions. High Scalability Long-running blog of real architecture case studies from engineering teams at scale.
🤝 Behavioral & HR Interviews
Pramp Free live peer-to-peer mock interviews — both technical and behavioral formats. Big Interview Structured video-based training built around the STAR method for behavioral answers. Glassdoor Interview Questions Real, company-specific interview questions and experiences reported by past candidates. The STAR Method Situation → Task → Action → Result — the standard framework for structuring behavioral answers.
📄 Resume & LinkedIn
Zety Resume and cover letter builder with ATS-friendly templates. Novoresume Clean, modern resume templates with built-in content suggestions. LinkedIn Learning Video courses on interviewing, resume writing, and personal branding.
💰 Salary & Offer Negotiation
Levels.fyi Crowdsourced compensation data by company and level — the standard for tech offer benchmarking. Glassdoor Salaries Broader coverage across industries, not just tech. Payscale Personalized pay reports based on your role, skills, and location.
📚 Books Worth Reading
Cracking the Coding Interview Gayle Laakmann McDowell — the classic reference for coding interview patterns and problems. Coding Designing Data-Intensive Applications Martin Kleppmann — deep, well-respected reading on the fundamentals behind system design. System Design Never Split the Difference Chris Voss — negotiation tactics from an FBI hostage negotiator, widely applied to salary talks. Negotiation The Pragmatic Programmer Hunt & Thomas — broad software craftsmanship habits that come up often in technical discussions. Technical
👥 Communities & Networking
r/cscareerquestions Active Reddit community for interview experiences, offer comparisons, and career advice. Blind Anonymous professional community popular for candid compensation and interview discussions. freeCodeCamp Free curriculum plus an active forum for developers at every stage.
