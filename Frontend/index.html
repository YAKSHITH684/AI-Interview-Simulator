About AI Interview Simulator

AI Interview Simulator is an intelligent interview preparation platform designed to help students and professionals improve their interview skills. The system combines Artificial Intelligence, Resume Analysis, and Interactive Mock Interviews to provide a complete preparation experience.

Users can upload their resumes, receive AI-powered feedback, analyze technical skills, practice HR and technical interviews, and track their overall readiness through a personalized dashboard.

The platform aims to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry expectations by offering real-world interview experiences in a safe and interactive environment.
